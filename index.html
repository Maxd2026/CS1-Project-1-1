Chocolate Chip Pancakes:

Description:

Easy to make homemade chocolate chip pancakes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup all purpose flower
  • 2 tbsp white sugar
  • 1tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 2tbsp butter
  • 1/2cup chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Add both bowls into a seperate bowl and stir until fully combined
  4. Pour about 1/4 cup of the batter onto the skillet for each pancake.
  5. Add chocolate chips as desired.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface (about 2-3 minutes), then flip
  7. Remove from the skillet and keep warm.
  8. Enjoy the pancakes

Scambled Eggs:

Description:

Delicious fluffy eggs to make at home!

Ingredients:

  • 2-4 large eggs (depending on desired serving)
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1tbsp milk or cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps:

  1. Carefully crack eggs into a mixing bowl
  2. Add the milk or cream (if using), and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Whisk the mixture until the eggs are fully combined and slightly frothy.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and add butter.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.
  6. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for 30 seconds.
  7. Gently stir the eggs, pushing them toward the center.
  8. Continue cooking, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are set.
  9. Remove the skillet from the heat and serve while hot.
